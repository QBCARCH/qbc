Founded in 2010, QBC has flourished in various creative fields, including architecture, urban planning, industrial design, and graphic design. 

Our rationale is based on blending essential laws of nature with modern design principles. We achieve this by employing a unique method seeking to create comprehensive solutions optimized to the user, regardless of complexity of initial restrictions and ultimate project impact.

Each project begins with a pattern-based research process. This process entails defining clients' expectations and using them to integrate appropriate design elements to reach an optimal balance between aesthetic and practicality in final design solutions

By understanding clients’ project goals and strategizing methods to achieve them in early phases, budgeting and timeliness are preserved and respected, and a more compelling building design is conceived to serve as the basis from which the final project grows. 

In the process of developing streamlined design solutions, we also aim to minimize energy costs in our design execution, thereby creating synergy between sustainability and functionality in each of our design endeavours.

Our approach

Deep Dive Discovery

In our architectural practice, we initiate each project by deeply immersing ourselves in the essence of your brand. Through comprehensive dialogues and meticulous research, we distill the core of your vision and identify the unique challenges inherent to your project.                                              

Strategic Creativity

Our team of designers and strategists collaborate seamlessly to develop concepts that are not only visually compelling but also strategically aligned with your business objectives. We transcend mere aesthetics to ensure that every design solution serves a functional purpose and supports your overarching goals.

Refined Execution

Drawing from a rich artistic heritage and the forefront of contemporary trends, we continually push the boundaries of design while maintaining an unwavering attention to detail. Our creations are both bold and refined, ensuring they leave a lasting impression on your audience.                                              

Immersive Motion Integration

We assert that motion is integral to enlivening our designs. From nuanced animations to comprehensive video productions, we seamlessly incorporate movement to craft immersive and engaging experiences.                                              

Collaborative Iteration

Your input is integral to our process. We maintain transparent and continuous communication throughout the project’s lifecycle, refining and iterating based on your feedback to ensure the final outcome not only meets but surpasses your expectations.                                              

Measurable Impact

We ensure our engagement extends beyond mere delivery. We equip you with the necessary tools and insights to evaluate the impact of our work, guaranteeing that our creative solutions yield tangible results for your business.
